Movie and Series

I have watched quite a lot of series and movie throughout my short life. One of my absolute favs is Dark Shadows featuring Johnny Depp and Elena Bonham Carter. This won't be everyone's cup of tea but it is a good option for anyone who wants to watch something entertaining and likes vampires and werewolves. I obviously watched all of the Twilight movies as well as Teen wolf, Bucky the vampire slayer, Vampire academy and My babysitter is a vampire. THese aren't for people who are very serious about a solid plot and want a super convincing acting from the performers. Honestly I feel like those are better suited for the daydreamers who watch them to get some inspo for future potential scenarios they'll make up in their mind. It is also obviously for the Wattpad lovers, ready to eat up all of the fanfictions that will emerge from it. A series about vampires I would recommend for people who enjoy solid plots is Castlevania: I LOVED IT and I found it super well-made.

My Favorite Lunch Spot

Why I love that place

I used to go there often with my friends back in high school as it was close to our school and was a good alternative to the usual fastfood chains available in Saint Lazare (KFC, Burger King and Macdonalds) and it cheap for a homemade burger place. TIPS: the restaurant itself is pretty small so if you want to eat inside and you're with friends you should send one to take your seats so that you'll have enough for the group (you'll notice that everyone does it so there is no need to culpabilize).

How to get there:

  1. Get to Saint-Lazare metro/train station: Metro 3, 12, 13 and 14 and Train lines J and L
  2. Head east on Rue Saint-Lazare toward Rue de Budapest (350 m)
  3. Turn right onto Rue de la Chau. of Antin (230 m)
  4. Turn left onto Rue de Provence (190 m)
  5. Turn right onto Rue Taitbout
  6. Destination will be on the right
